Lambda Zulu / Artists / grassbloc
grassbloc
Producer · Composer

Lo-fi and instrumental work rooted in mood and atmosphere. grassbloc makes releases that feel like specific times of day — the particular quality of light at 11pm, the way a Tuesday in autumn sits differently than anything else. Warm, reflective, and unhurried.

Two EPs on Lambda Zulu, both mapping the same emotional territory from different angles. Music for commutes that feel longer than they are, for rooms you don't want to leave yet.
