Lambda Zulu / Artists / Ceesh
Ceesh
Producer · Instrumentalist

Patient, textured work that sits somewhere between ambient and beat music. Ceesh builds instrumentals that feel inhabited — not background music, but music that occupies a space and stays there. Built for late nights, empty rooms, and the long way home.

Active since the early releases on Lambda Zulu, Ceesh has developed a solo voice that's consistently unhurried and deliberately toned, drawing on texture and structure rather than spectacle.
